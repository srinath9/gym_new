DESCRIPTION: Get a bird's eyeview of the fathoming seas and greens of Goa, as you go Hang Gliding. Let wind ruffle your hair and brush past against your face, as you hover around in a Delta Plane, commonly known as Hang Glider. With the lush settings and gurgling waters of Goa forming a perfect backdrop, your adrenaline will set soaring. The thermal drifts at Goa are nothing less than the most ideal conditions for hang gliding. Usually safe, Hang Gliding is quite an easy sport, and a must for the nomads around. All the equipment and necessary training is provided by the operators. Discover a whole new side of Goa, as you glide away from the humdrum of parties and beach life here. Fly high!
